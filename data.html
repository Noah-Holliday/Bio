We're doing charts on the government of Indiana's spending, in hopes of giving the people of Indiana an idea of how their tax money is being spent.

Government Spending

The money that the goverment collects through taxes are used for funding, and so the people of the country have a right to know where that money goes. The following charts give data on where their money is being spent, keep in mind however that the information we have only lists percentages, and not the total amount of money collected throughout the year.

The charts above provide data on how the goverment spends it's money, do you think that perhaps the goverment spends too much money in some locations, or perhaps the goverment spends too little on items that you may consider much more important to fund than something else on the list. Either way these charts were made so you could ask questions like this, and come to conclusions on your own.
