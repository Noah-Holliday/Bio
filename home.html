Indianapolis is a great place to live, with a striving and fast growing technology field. Thousands of new technology jobs have surfaced in recent years, nearly doubling since 2010. Indy has scored over $7 billion in acqusitions and IPO's in mainly software companies over the last decade. One of Indy's main challenges moving forward is a lack technology talent and it's inability to retain the talent it does generate; being one of the main exporters of promising graduates. Luckily new programs have been popping up to remedy just this problem; programs such as Nextech that both cultivates and tries to retain said talent. They do this using a six week program which includes four weeks of back-end developing along with two weeks of commuity service and tours to show the value of staying.
