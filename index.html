At the time I never thought I would be interested in computers, I didn't even decide to take the class. I was just automatically placed into the class and it was just another credit to me. However, throughout the year as we did fun and challenging activities I realized I could see myself doing this ten years from now. It was the first time I have ever felt something like that, and I have been pursuing it ever since.
